Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Formation: Teams can consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Project Originality: All projects must be built from scratch during the 24-hour hackathon period.
  • Code of Conduct: Be respectful, inclusive, and collaborative. No harassment will be tolerated.
  • Submission: Submit a link to a public code repository (e.g., GitHub) and a short demo video.
  • Judging Criteria: Projects judged on technical complexity, innovation, design, and presentation.
  • Intellectual Property: You own what you build.
  • Final Decision: The decisions of the judges are final.
